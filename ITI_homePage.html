About our ITI

Industrial Training Institute are Training Institute which provide training in technical field and constituted under Directorate General of Employment & Training (DGET) Ministry of Labor & Employment,Union Government of India. Normally a person who has passed 10th standard (SSLC) is eligible for admission to ITI, The objective of opening of ITI is provide technical manpower to Industries.
